Popular Dishes

Pancake

Breakfast

75 peso

Ingredients:
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
Instructions:
  1. Sift together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat eggs and add milk.
  3. Combine all ingredients, mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a pan and pour batter to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until golden brown on both sides. Serve warm.

Halo-halo

Dessert

95 peso

Ingredients:
  • Crushed ice
  • Milk
  • Leche flan
  • Sago
  • Ube jam
  • Sweet beans
Instructions:
  1. Layer sweet beans, sago, nata de coco, and ube in a tall glass.
  2. Add crushed ice on top.
  3. Pour evaporated milk over the ice.
  4. Top with leche flan and scoop of ube jam.
  5. Mix well before eating for the best taste.

Bistek

Main Dish

80 peso

Ingredients:
  • Beef
  • Soy sauce
  • Calamansi
  • Onion
  • Pepper
Instructions:
  1. Marinate beef in soy sauce, calamansi juice, and pepper for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry onions until soft. Set aside.
  3. Cook marinated beef in the same pan until tender.
  4. Return onions to the pan and simmer for a few more minutes.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice.
